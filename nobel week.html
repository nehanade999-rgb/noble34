What is Nobel Week?

Nobel Week is held every December in Stockholm and Oslo to honor the Nobel Prize laureates. The week includes lectures, press meetings, cultural events, and concludes with the grand Nobel Prize Award Ceremony and the Nobel Banquet.

Nobel Week Schedule

Date Event Location
December 6 Opening of Nobel Week & Press Conference Nobel Prize Museum, Stockholm
December 7 Laureate Lectures in Physics and Chemistry Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences
December 8 Medicine and Literature Lectures Karolinska Institute & Stockholm University
December 9 Peace Prize Ceremony Rehearsal Oslo City Hall, Norway
December 10 Nobel Prize Award Ceremony & Banquet Stockholm Concert Hall & City Hall

Week Highlights

Laureate Lectures

Each Nobel laureate shares insights into their groundbreaking discoveries, open to students and researchers worldwide.

Cultural Events

Exhibitions, concerts, and film screenings inspired by science, literature, and peace — showcasing global creativity.

Nobel Banquet

The grand finale, held in Stockholm City Hall, featuring royal guests, laureates, and a world-class dining experience.

The Nobel Foundation

Tasked with a mission to manage Alfred Nobel’s fortune and has ultimate responsibility for fulfilling the intentions of Nobel’s will.
