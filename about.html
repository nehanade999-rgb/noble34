About the Nobel Prize

Established in 1901, the Nobel Prize honors exceptional contributions to humanity in fields such as Physics, Chemistry, Medicine, Literature, Peace, and Economic Sciences. The awards celebrate individuals and organizations who have inspired progress, knowledge, and peace across the globe.

Inspired by Alfred Nobel’s vision, the Nobel Prize remains a symbol of excellence and dedication to making the world a better place through innovation, compassion, and intellect.
