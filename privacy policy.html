Introduction

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

  • Personal information you provide, such as your name, email, and contact details.
  • Information automatically collected, including your IP address, browser type, and usage patterns.
  • Cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our website services.
  • To communicate with you regarding updates or newsletters.
  • To ensure security and prevent unauthorized access.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted partners to provide website functionality or as required by law.

Cookies

We use cookies to personalize content, provide social media features, and analyze traffic. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You may also object to certain processing of your data. Contact us using the details below for any requests.

Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@nobel.org.
