Visitor Information

Tickets & Entry

Tickets are issued by the Nobel Foundation and are required for all guests. Bring valid ID for entry.

Transport

Nearest metro: Hötorget. Limited parking; please use public transport. Arrive at least 45 minutes early.

Live Broadcast & Media

The ceremony will be streamed live through official Nobel channels. Press and media registration is open on the Nobel Foundation website.

Dress Code & Etiquette

Formal black tie or national attire is required. Maintain decorum and turn off mobile devices during the ceremony.
