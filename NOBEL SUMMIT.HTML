About the Summit

The Nobel Summit unites Nobel Laureates, global policymakers, scientists, and young innovators to discuss urgent global challenges such as climate change, peacebuilding, and sustainable development. The 2025 Summit will focus on “Science for a Shared Future.”

Summit Details

Date

April 10–12, 2025

Location

Washington, D.C., USA

Theme

Science for a Shared Future

Summit Schedule

Day Session Topic
Day 1 Opening Ceremony Welcome Address and Keynote by Nobel Laureates
Day 2 Panel Discussions Climate Action • Peace & Conflict • AI & Ethics
Day 3 Innovation Forum Global Youth Innovation Challenge & Closing Remarks

Summit Highlights

Global Collaboration

Experts from over 50 countries will collaborate to share ideas for global improvement.

Youth Innovation

Young innovators will present sustainable projects to Nobel Laureates and international leaders.

Future Vision

The summit will conclude with a declaration on building a peaceful and sustainable planet.
